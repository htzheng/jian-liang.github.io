About Myself

I am currently an associate professor at Center for Research on Intelligent Perception and Computing (CRIPAC) affiliated to the Institute of Automation, Chinese Academy of Sciences (CAS). From June 2019 to May 2021, I was a research fellow at the Vision and Learning Group, National University of Singapore (NUS), working with Jiashi Feng. Before joining NUS in 2019, I obtained Ph.D. in Pattern Recognition and Intelligent Systems from Institute of Automation, Chinese Academy of Sciences (CASIA) in Jan 2019, under the supervision of Tieniu Tan and co-supervision of Ran He and Zhenan Sun, and received my bachelor degree in Automation from Xi'an Jiaotong University in June 2013.

My current research interests mainly focus on deep representation learning, unsupervised/ self-supervised learning and transfer learning (including domain adaptation), with their applications to vision and multimedia problems.
